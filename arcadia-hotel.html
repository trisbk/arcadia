Welcome to Arcadia

A calm retreat in a city that never slows down

Set on Jalan Pramuka in Sario Kotabaru, Arcadia Hotel Manado places you minutes from Megamas Boulevard, Manado Town Square and the waterfront — yet behind our doors the pace softens. Modern rooms, considered lighting and genuine Minahasan warmth make every stay feel effortless.

Whether you arrive for diving in Bunaken, a business trip, or a weekend by the Celebes Sea, our team looks after the details so you can simply enjoy Manado.

Explore Manado

North Sulawesi

Reefs, ridges and a city by the sea

Manado is the gateway to Bunaken's legendary walls and a city that comes alive after dark along the waterfront. From Arcadia, the best of it is a short ride away.

🌶Bold flavours
Local Culinary Experiences

Taste Minahasa

Manado's kitchen is famously fiery and fresh. Try tinutuan (Manado porridge) for breakfast, smoky cakalang fufu, and grilled reef fish dabbed in dabu-dabu sambal. Our concierge will point you to the best warung and night markets.

Prefer to stay in? Saba Restaurant serves a curated Minahasan menu every evening.
